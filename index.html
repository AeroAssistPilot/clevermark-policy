We use the RECORD_AUDIO permission to enable voice commands and AI interactions between your Wear OS device and mobile phone.

Audio data is used exclusively to process your requests via AI. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

Important: We do not store, save, or share your voice recordings. Audio is processed for the immediate request and is deleted immediately after the AI response is generated. No personal voice data is kept on our servers.
